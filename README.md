# CS2-Simplify-Radar-Settings


  <p>Simple way to modify ur radar</p>
<br>
<p>Download the config and put it: <pre>...\steamapps\common\Counter-Strike Global Offensive\game\csgo\cfg</pre></p>
<h3>The Settings:</h3>
<blockquote>
  <i>RCenter[0 or 1] / RCentered[0 or 1]</i> - <code>Centering on player (togglable)</code>
  <br><i>RRotate[0 or 1]</i> - <code>Rotating by player's view (togglable)</code>
  <br><i>RSDynamic[0 or 1]</i> - <code>ON/OFF dynamic scaling of radar (togglable)</code>
  <br><i>RIScale[num]</i> - <code>Icon Size (+0.1 and .25 / .75 until 2)</code>
  <br><i>RSAlt[0 or 1]</i> - <code>Alternative radar size (togglable)</code>
  <br><i>RAdapt[0 or 1] / RAddictive[0 or 1]</i> - <code>Radar(<i>miniMap</i>) adapt to map (togglable)</code>
  <br><i>RScale[num]</i> - <code>Radar scale (+0.1 and .25 / .75 until 2)</code>
  <br><i>RZoom[num]</i> - <code>Radar zoom (+0.1 and .25 / .75 until 2 [and 0.35])</code>
</blockquote>
<h5>Square format</h5>
<blockquote>
  <i>RSquare[0 or 1]</i> - <code>Always square radar (togglable)</code>
  <br><i>RSquareTAB[0 or 1]</i> - <code>Square radar in scoreboard (togglable)</code>
</blockquote>
<h5>BG or radar</h5>
<blockquote>
  <i>RBAlpha[num]</i> - <code>Radar bg alpha(from 0 to 1, only 0.1)</code>
  <br><i>RBBlur[0 or 1]</i> - <code>Radar bg blur(togglable)</code>
</blockquote>
